About Us

Paper Fetish caters to you, the millennial notebook-lover, to satisfy your professional needs without sacrificing your individualistic tastes.

We have launched three quality lines of notebooks in the form of minimalistic design, aesthetic colour palettes and unique lettering, each with its own personal touch.

Our Story

The Paper Fetish story is simple. It started with the idea of creating high quality notebooks, ready to deal with any type of use without a splotch of ink bleeding through the paper.

Our brand was founded in 2017, and it all began with a semi-serious obsession with notebooks. Hence, the name Paper Fetish was born and our story began.

Our Mission

The Paper Fetish mission is to share & promote a love for notebooks in people through our unique and functional designs, whilst always seeking continuous innovation within our products and respecting Mother Nature in the process.

Our Process

Design : Our minimalistic designs draw inspiration from daily living, from laughter and youth, and the beautiful city of Singapore.

Assembly : Only the most high quality and refined materials have been used to craft our creations. All products are thoroughly supervised during production and this takes place by hand at our workshop in Singapore Poly.

Inspection : Every notebook is carefully inspected before being shipped into your bookbag.

Packaging : The packaging takes place at our workshop at Singapore Poly, Singapore. Each book is wrapped by hand, and sent with love.
